## 🚀 Overview

OrchIDE is a state-of-the-art, AI-native Integrated Development Environment (IDE) built to accelerate software development workflows. Combining the familiarity of industry-standard coding environments with cutting-edge autonomous agents, OrchIDE actively assists, refactors, and writes code alongside you. 

Designed as a modern SaaS solution, OrchIDE seamlessly syncs your preferences, intelligently indexes your context, and securely connects you with the most advanced AI models available—allowing you to focus on logic rather than boilerplate.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Agentic Assistance:** Move beyond basic autocomplete. OrchIDE's autonomous agents can understand full repositories, execute complex multi-file refactoring, and debug intelligently.
- **Deep Contextual Awareness:** The editor maintains an active understanding of your workspace, local dependencies, and framework context to provide hyper-relevant suggestions.
- **Cloud-Native Sync:** Your settings, customizable layouts, and AI configurations are securely backed up to the cloud and synchronized across all your devices.
- **Enterprise-Grade Security:** We prioritize your code's privacy. Authentication is robust, and your proprietary source code is never retained or used to train external models.
- **High-Performance Architecture:** Optimized for absolute speed, OrchIDE handles massive monorepos without lag, utilizing a state-of-the-art native rendering engine.

## 🛠️ Getting Started

### 1. Download & Install
Download the latest version of OrchIDE for your operating system (macOS or Windows) from our [official releases page](https://github.com/sameer786ss/OrchIDE/releases).

### 2. Authentication
Launch OrchIDE and sign in to your account. A secure initialization flow will authenticate your session, verify your subscription, and sync your workspace.

### 3. Initialize a Workspace
Drag and drop your project folder directly into the window, or select "Open Project." OrchIDE will automatically begin background indexing to power local AI capabilities.

## 💻 System Requirements

- **Operating System:** Windows 10/11 (x64) or macOS 12.0+ (Intel & Apple Silicon)
- **Memory:** 8 GB RAM (16 GB strongly recommended for large, complex workspaces)
- **Network:** Broadband internet connection required for agentic features and cloud-sync
